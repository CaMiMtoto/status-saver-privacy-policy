Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Status Saver application (“App”). The App is designed to help users view and save status images and videos from messaging applications such as WhatsApp.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The App may access media files (images and videos) stored on your device only for the purpose of displaying and saving WhatsApp statuses that are already visible to the user.

3. How We Use Information

All media accessed by the App remains on your device. The App does not upload, transmit, or share any data to external servers.

4. Permissions

The App may request storage or media permissions solely to:

  • Read WhatsApp status images and videos
  • Save selected statuses to your device

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, all data remains under the control of the user’s device.

6. Third-Party Services

This App does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or external data collection services.

7. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children or any users.
